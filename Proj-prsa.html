PRSA's Mission Statement:

The goal of PRSA is to unite the Puerto Rican students at UCF and to help and support each other throughout the educational journey. PRSA stands for opportunity. The organization helps its members excel at UCF by providing them with proper leadership and personal growth experiences. PRSA will offer a safe, comfortable and judgement free environment for Puerto Rican students to come together and smoothly adjust to the campus life. Discussions about personal experiences and cultural diversity will serve as the primary source for developing harmony and friendship among all of its members and the rest of the UCF student body. PRSA, as the acting Puerto Rican institution at UCF, will look after the educational and social development and progress of the Puerto Rican community in Orlando and Central Florida.

My Role:

As Marketing Director for the Puerto Rican Student Association at UCF, I led targeted social media campaigns to promote cultural events and foster a sense of community for Puerto Rican students. By managing Instagram and creating engaging content, I successfully increased our online following by 200 new members while driving awareness for our initiatives. Additionally, I built partnerships with media outlets and other Latin student organizations, enhancing the visibility of our events and strengthening connections across the campus community. Through these efforts, I helped create an environment where students could connect, celebrate their culture, and feel at home.
